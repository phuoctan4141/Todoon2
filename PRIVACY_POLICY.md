# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Todoon ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you register using email and password (via Firebase Authentication), we collect:
- Email address
- User ID

### 1.2 Task Data
We store:
- Task titles and descriptions
- Attached images (if any)
- Timestamps
- Completion status

### 1.3 Device Information
We may collect limited technical information necessary for:
- Push notifications
- App performance and security

## 2. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using:

- **Hive** (local storage on your device)
- **Firebase Firestore** (cloud database by Google)

Firebase services may process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

## 3. Image Access

The App may request access to your device storage to allow you to:

- Select images to attach to tasks

We do NOT:
- Automatically scan your gallery
- Upload images without your action

Images are only processed when you choose to attach them.

## 4. Notifications

We may send push notifications for:

- Task reminders
- App updates

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

## 5. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the App
- Sync your tasks across devices
- Improve performance and reliability
- Send reminders and updates

We do NOT sell your personal data.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data except:

- With Firebase (Google) to provide authentication and cloud storage
- When required by law

## 7. Data Retention

Your data is retained:

- As long as your account is active
- Until you delete your account

When you delete your account, associated cloud data may be permanently removed.

## 8. Security

We rely on Firebase security infrastructure and industry-standard practices to protect your data.

However, no system is 100% secure.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Todoon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request deletion
- Request correction

Contact us to exercise these rights.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
